About

Fiyinfoluwa Adeleke is Associate General Counsel and Head of International Privacy at Yahoo, where he oversees privacy and data protection programmes across Europe, Asia-Pacific, and Latin America. He works at the intersection of regulation and compliance, advising on recommendation systems, automated decision-making, and algorithmic transparency under GDPR and the EU AI Act. Fiyinfoluwa represents Yahoo in regulatory matters with data protection authorities across multiple jurisdictions.

Previously at Hertz, he led data protection compliance programmes across EMEA and Asia-Pacific. He also worked in financial services at Equiniti, bringing experience from a highly regulated sector to technology privacy challenges.

A solicitor qualified in Ireland and the UK, and a Barrister & Solicitor in Nigeria, he brings international regulatory expertise to complex technology privacy challenges. Fiyinfoluwa speaks regularly at privacy conferences, including Dentons Privacy Academy and Privacy+Security Forum, on AI governance and cross-border compliance. He holds FIP, CIPM, CIPP/E, and GRCP certifications.
